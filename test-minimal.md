# MCM USV Connectivity and Communications Guide

## Executive Summary
The Mine Countermeasures Unmanned Surface Vehicle (MCM USV) utilizes a highly distributed, multi-layered communications architecture.

This guide provides a completely contained narrative of how the USV's "brain" and "nervous system" communicate. 
*   **For Non-Developers:** This guide explains how the hardware nodes fit together.
*   **For Developers:** This guide serves as the ultimate technical reference.
